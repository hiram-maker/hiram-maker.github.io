About Flurry Powders

Flurry Powders specializes in porous‑particle spray drying and advanced formulation development for dry‑powder inhalation and nasal delivery systems. The team brings decades of experience developing successful inhalation products and provides R&D‑focused PSD1 spray drying, analytical testing, and formulation consulting tailored specifically for inhaled therapeutics.

Our multidisciplinary expertise spans formulation, colloidal science, aerosol physics, materials science, and device engineering. This technical foundation has supported multiple FDA‑approved and late‑stage inhalation programs utilizing porous‑particle spray‑drying platforms such as PulmoSpheres™.

Flurry’s bespoke PSD1 system produces powders with superior aerosol performance and higher yields compared to conventional CDMO spray drying lines. Our GMP‑aligned R&D workflow enables rapid iteration, advanced analytical testing, and IND‑ready technical packages prior to full GMP tech transfer.
