About Flurry Powders

Flurry Powders specializes in porous‑particle spray drying and advanced formulation development for dry‑powder inhalation and nasal delivery systems. The team brings decades of experience developing successful inhalation products and offers R&D‑focused PSD1 spray drying, analytical testing, and formulation consulting services.

Flurry combines deep expertise across formulation, colloidal science, aerosol physics, materials science, and device engineering. This approach has been validated in multiple FDA‑approved and late‑stage inhalation programs utilizing porous‑particle spray‑drying platforms such as PulmoSpheres™.

Our bespoke PSD1 spray‑drying system produces powders with superior aerosol performance and higher yields than standard CDMO equipment. The R&D‑driven, GMP‑aligned workflow supports rapid iteration, advanced analytical testing, and IND‑ready technical packages prior to full GMP tech transfer.
