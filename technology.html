Technology

Services — Flurry Powders provides end‑to‑end R&D for inhalable therapeutics, combining advanced particle engineering, formulation expertise, process development, and analytical excellence to produce aerosol medicines that meet client specifications and regulatory requirements — helping partners accelerate timelines, reduce risk, and improve patient outcomes.
